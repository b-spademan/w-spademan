About

After teaching music privately and with the Toronto District School Board for over 30 years, I decided to combine my experience as an educator with my passion for physical fitness and healthy living. In early 2020 I completed the training required by Canfitpro to provide Personal Training and Weight Loss Coaching services. Now I am very excited to share my expertise and work with people to help them achieve their goals of healthy, active aging!

Personal Assessment

Before designing your personal fitness program, I will conduct a thorough assessment of your current fitness level, including strength, cardiorespiratory capacity, flexibility and body composition.

Together we will set realistic goals to improve your health and physical fitness.

Based on your priorities, I will plan a series of workouts and adapt them to meet your needs.

Fitness Routines

I offer one-hour sessions in my home studio, your home or virtually. Your workout will begin with a light cardio warmup and some dynamic stretching.

Then we will do a series of strength training exercises focused on your individual needs.

We will finish with some full body stretches that will leave you feeling physically and mentally energized.

Diet

We will meet for two or three one hour sessions to analyze your eating habits and dietary needs.

I will then support you in making nutritionally sound and practical changes that will lead to weight loss.

Rather than asking you to adhere to a rigid diet we will develop personalized strategies that will allow you to make lasting, healthy changes to your food choices.
